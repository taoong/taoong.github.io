What is Sprout?

In collaboration with the Greater Good Science Center and the Tang Center, Sprout is aimed at promoting the knowledge and practice of wellness-oriented behaviors among the UC Berkeley student body and staff.



What it'll look like

Users plant a tree that dynamically symbolizes their wellness growth. This branch will have branches which represent the fundamental tenets of wellness: gratitude, kindness, respect, empathy, purpose, awe, etc. Ssers will be able to select activities that relate to these “branches”, perform these activities, and grow their tree. Some activities include meditation or showing gratitude; we plan to be working with the “Greater Good in Action” applet: https://ggia.berkeley.edu/.

Features

  • Customize Your Journey

    Our questionaire will identify your wellness strengths and recommend activities to focus on

  • Track Your Progress

    Visualize your wellness journey with the growth of your tree branches whenever you complete an activity

  • Connect With Others

    Send letters of gratitude, attend wellness-oriented events on-campus, and encourage others on their progress
