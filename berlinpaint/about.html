More About Berlin Paint.

Berlin Co. Ltd. established in Taiwan in 1962. In 2000, Berlin expanded to establish in Mainland China. Through meticulous selection process, Berlin (Hui-Zhou) Scientific Chemical Co., Ltd. the first fully owned overseas manufacturing operation facility of Berlin Co. Ltd., settles at Huizhou City, Guangdong, China. The total asset is over 4 million US dollars, occupying over 30,000 square meters.

Berlin (Hui-Zhou) formed a joint venture with RED SPOT of USA to manufacture advanced insulating coatings for magnetic ring and sports equipment coatings.

In 2004: Berlin (Hui-Zhou) begins OEM for Cafco of USA to produce its fireproofing coating. Berlin continues the relationship with Promat after its merge with Cafco. August 2004: Berlin (Hui-Zhou) forms a joint venture with Atlas Mineral & Chemical Company of USA, to manufacture its industrial strength anti-corrosion products.
